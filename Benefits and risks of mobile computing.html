Benefits Risks
-Increased responsiveness to customers'needs and requests. -Unless they are adequately protected,these mobile devicies can increase the risk of confidential information being accessed by attackers.
-Increased employee productivity by allowing employees greater access to company resources. -New or changing platforms can require additional security controls to ensure data remains secure.
-Developing efficiencies through the use of new applications. -New platforms and the devices on which they operate can highlight vulnerabilities within the corporation's infrastructure.
